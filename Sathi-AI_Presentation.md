# Sathi-AI: Elderly Care Assistant
*AI-Powered Voice Companion for Senior Citizens*

## 🎯 Problem Statement

**The Growing Challenge of Elderly Care**

- **Social Isolation**: 1 in 4 elderly adults experience loneliness, leading to depression and health decline
- **Medication Non-Adherence**: 50% of seniors fail to take medications as prescribed, causing hospital readmissions
- **Emergency Response Delays**: Seniors living alone face critical delays in receiving emergency assistance
- **Technology Barrier**: Complex interfaces prevent elderly users from adopting beneficial technology
- **Cognitive Load**: Remembering daily tasks, appointments, and health routines becomes challenging with age

**Real Impact**: Millions of seniors struggle with independence, safety, and connection in their daily lives.

- **Daily Assistance**: Time, weather, and information services

- **Database**: SQLite (lightweight, reliable, no server needed, perfect for local deployment)
